Who We Are

Hello all! My name is Anton Zapolskyi, founder of Tradelize. The foundation of Tradelize stands on my profound fund raising experience.

My experience

At one time I traded through several American brokers, and all I could do to raise funds for management was print out balance sheets from my brokers and take them to people I knew, or people who knew people I knew, waving around my print-out saying, “Look how successful I am! Give me your money for management and we’ll profit together!”

Our Vision.

Think a new way of the wealth management! No hidden fees, third parties, complexities. You always know where your money is and remain in full control of it.

Speed.

An instant access and flawless execution on global capital markets via Tradelize Terminal

Interaction.

Ability to interact with investors to share new ideas, compare performance and scale their potential by raising funds for management via Investment Portal

Productivity.

Powerful toolset to unite into communities and proprietary trading companies to amplify their performance or mitigate losses of their mates
